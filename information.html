Why are my friends and family mysteriously dying?

This is disease known as the Black Death, contagious and deadly.

What is a disease?

A disease is a contagious condition.

You may have heard of a "Great Pestilence" carving its way through Europe.

It is extremely contagious, as just touching clothes of the sick and infect you.

It is also extremely efficient, as a healthy person can go to bed and be dead by morning.

How can I avoid it?

There are several ways you can avoid it

  • Wash your hands
  • Social distance (stay away from infected or people you may suspect to be infected)
  • Quarantine (if you are infected or suspect you are infected, stay away from others)

Am I sick?

If you have any of these symptoms, quarantine yourself IMMEDIATELY

  • Fever
  • Headache
  • Chills
  • Weakness
  • Painful lymph nodes
